How to Return an Item

1

Initiate Return

Sign in and go to Order History. Select the order and click "Return Items"

2

Select Items & Reason

Choose which items to return and provide a brief reason for the return

3

Print Return Label

Download and print the prepaid return shipping label from your email

4

Package & Ship

Securely pack the item(s) and attach the label. Drop off at carrier or schedule pickup

5

Receive Refund

Track your return and receive refund confirmation once processed

🔄

Exchanges & Special Cases

Exchanges

Want a different size or color? Exchanges are processed as soon as we receive your return and ship immediately.

Damaged Items

Received a damaged item? Contact support immediately with photos. We'll send a replacement or refund right away.

Wrong Item

If we sent the wrong item, we'll send the correct one at no charge and provide a prepaid label for the return.

Late Returns

Returns after 30 days may be accepted as store credit at our discretion. Contact support to discuss.

⚠️

Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened personal care items (cosmetics, skincare, hygiene products)
  • Digital products and gift cards
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" at purchase
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Perishable goods
